This book explains how AI competitions and benchmarks are created, run, and used. It brings together lessons from experienced organizers in academia, industry, and non-profits. Covering topics like datasets, evaluation, platforms, and incentives, it shows how challenges drive research, education, and innovation. Designed for researchers, engineers, and organizers, it is a practical guide to understanding and building impactful AI competitions : [book URL](https://ai-competitions-book.github.io/)
